=== SLIDE 3 ===
EQUINE RECURRENT
UVEITIS (ERU)
Definition
Chronic, recurrent inflammation ofthe uveal tractAffects iris, ciliary body, choroidLeading cause of blindness inhorses

=== SLIDE 4 ===
EQUINE RECURRENT
UVEITIS (ERU)
Pathophysiology 
Immune-mediated disease
Breakdown of ocular immune
privilege
Autoantibodies attack eye tissues
Triggered by Leptospira
interrogans or trauma

=== SLIDE 5 ===
EQUINE RECURRENT
UVEITIS (ERU)
Signalment
All breeds affected
Appaloosas = highest risk
(genetic)
Any age; often young to
middle-aged horses
